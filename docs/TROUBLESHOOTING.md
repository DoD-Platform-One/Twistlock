In addition to normal operational issues that you can encounter with any distributed application, since Twistlock is meant to block certain actions on a machine by design, sometimes it can interfere with intentional actions you are trying to take on your cluster, e.g. upgrading OpenShift. To help deal with this, it may be useful to review debug logs. Information about accessing debug logs is available [here](https://docs.prismacloud.io/en/compute-edition/34/admin-guide/howto/review-debug-logs).
